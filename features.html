Study Buddie: The Unified Platform

Comprehensive Features: Visibility, ERP & Communication

Study Buddie is a single, intelligent platform that brings together school administration, pedagogy, and communication, providing immense value to every stakeholder.

AI Learning Scrutiny

Deep, predictive analysis of student effort and engagement patterns. Understand how students learn, not just what they score.

Predictive Intervention

Use machine learning to identify at-risk students weeks in advance. Shift from remedial action to proactive guidance to maximize potential.

Self-Study Tracking

Track and verify student self-study time outside of school hours, providing measurable data on commitment and accountability.

Full ERP Automation

Automate complex processes like Fee collection, Live Attendance, HR management, and Asset Management to reduce administrative workload by up to 40%.

Dynamic Examination Module

Simplify exam scheduling, grading input, and the automated, customizable generation of professional report cards.

Library & Asset Manager

Efficiently manage all physical and digital resources with digital cataloging and quick issue/return functionality using Student Digital IDs.

Secure Parent-Teacher Chat

A secure, private, and traceable communication platform to foster direct, focused discussions on student growth between parents and educators.

Digital Timetable & Tasks

Real-time access for students to class schedules, assignment submissions, and instant notifications for homework deadlines.
