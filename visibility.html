The Innovation

Unlocking the Black Box of Learning: The Study Buddie Scrutiny Advantage

Study Buddie's core philosophy is complete transparency. It goes beyond traditional systems by combining ERP efficiency with real-time learning visibility. By connecting classroom data with home study insights, it builds complete transparency between schools, teachers, and parents.

01 Core Feature 01

Self-Study Tracking

Tracks every student's real study effort beyond school hours. Uses smart timers and logs to record subject-wise self-study sessions, giving teachers and parents measurable insights. Monitor and motivate student self-learning journeys with detailed tracking of time spent on resources, progress through modules, and quiz performance — empowering students to take ownership of their academic growth.

02 Core Feature 02

Transparent Parent Dashboard

Turns invisible learning into visible data for parents. Automatically syncs student study hours, attendance, and performance into a clean, real-time parent view. Provide parents with comprehensive insights into their child's academic performance, attendance, and behavioral trends — fostering a collaborative environment between home and school with unparalleled visibility.

Request a Demo
03 Core Feature 03

Integrated ERP + Learning Analytics

Merges academic data with behavior and effort analytics. Connects ERP attendance, fees, and grades with study performance to give a 360° learning profile. Unify core school operations with intelligent learning analytics to streamline administration and enhance educational outcomes — gaining a holistic view of institutional efficiency and individual student success.
